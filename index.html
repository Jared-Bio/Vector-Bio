A simple, low-friction process

You don’t need a “digital transformation.” You need the next 3–5 changes that remove the most drag.

  • 1) Discovery Call (free) — quick context, pain points, and constraints.
  • 2) Mini Audit — light data gathering: docs, tools, workflows, handoffs.
  • 3) Report — prioritized recommendations with effort/impact and rollout plan.
  • 4) Implementation — templates, SOPs, tool setup, training, and adoption.
  • 5) Stabilize — weekly tweaks until it runs without thinking about it.
  • 6) Optional Ongoing — monthly check-ins and continuous improvement.
